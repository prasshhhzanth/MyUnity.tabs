# MyUnity – Smart Community Management Platform

## Overview

MyUnity is a modern community management platform designed for gated communities, residential colonies, and welfare associations. The platform simplifies community operations by providing a centralized digital ecosystem for residents, administrators, security personnel, and service providers.

The application focuses on improving communication, security, convenience, and operational efficiency through a mobile-first experience.

---

## Problem Statement

Many residential communities still rely on WhatsApp groups, manual registers, phone calls, and fragmented systems to manage visitors, announcements, complaints, and community services. These traditional approaches often lead to communication gaps, inefficient operations, and poor user experience.

MyUnity aims to solve these challenges through a unified digital platform.
